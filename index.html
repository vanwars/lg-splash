Tell Your Community's Story

Local Ground is a free, open source online mapping tool that elevates community voices into compelling, digital stories.

Do you have a story to tell?

How It Works

Collect location data with your team

Layer visualizations, images, and drawings to tell a story

Use your map to advocate for ideas, solve problems, and create change

Make Maps

Create story maps that take your visitor on a journey through your data

  • Personalize

    Choose your map, map marker style, fonts and colors

  • Visualize Data

    Specify data fields to be collected and displayed. Add photos, audio files and videos to any place.

  • Incorporate sketches and images

    Overlay hand drawings and images on your map

  • Map your places

    Add places by searching for locations or drawing them directly on the map

Collaborate

Invite others to participate in your project

  • Work together

    Multiple people or remote teams can work concurrently on a map

  • Share data

    Individual maps can be created showing different views of a jointly made dataset

  • Learn data science

    Great for classroom or community-based projects

Share

Publish your maps to a select group or to the whole world

  • Publish your map

    Share a link to your map or embed maps in your website or blog

  • Decide who can see the map

    You have the option of requiring a password to view your map.
